Privacy policy

We respect your privacy and we are committed to protecting and respecting your personal data. We collect user data for the purpose of facilitating inventory management on behalf of the user i.e.
1) Creating new listings on behalf of the user,
2) Updating listings on behalf of the user,
3) Deleting listings on behalf of the user.
We necessarily hold user data for the duration that these actions are being performed and discard it immediatelly after.
